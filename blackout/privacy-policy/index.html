1. Introduction

Welcome to Blackout. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our habit tracking journal app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: We do not require personal information to use Blackout.
  • Usage Data: We do not collect anonymized data about app usage.
  • Journal Entries: Your habit data and journal entries are stored locally on your device and are never shared with us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We do not collect any usage data, analytics, or personal information. All interactions stay entirely on your device.

4. Data Security

We prioritize your privacy. All your data is stored securely on your device. We recommend enabling device-level security (such as a passcode or biometric lock) for additional protection.

5. Third-Party Services

Blackout does not share your data with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@projectout.app.

8. Your Rights

As we do not collect or store any personal information, there is no data for you to access, modify, or delete through our systems. All data remains under your full control on your own device.
